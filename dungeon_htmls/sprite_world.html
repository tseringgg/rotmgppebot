The Sprite World is a low to medium-level dungeon intended for moderately powerful (level 20 unmaxed) characters. The enemies are weak and have low health but the boss can be dangerous.



The dungeon boss drops Potions of Dexterity and rarely, Potions of Defense. It is considered to be the main source of Potions of Dexterity in the game. The dungeon is also the primary source of four untiered items: the Sprite Wand, Staff of Extreme Prejudice, Cloak of the Planewalker, and Mace of the Celestial Forest. It is also one of the locations where the Tarot Card Pack can be found to get a chance to obtain anyone of the Tarot Card Artifacts.



Portal to the Sprite World has a chance to drop from Sprite Beast and Sprite Child. It is also guaranteed to drop from Celestial Sprite and Cosmic Sprite.



This dungeon must be completed to earn ‘King of the Mountains‘, ‘Hero of the Nexus’ and ‘Realm of the Mad God’ fame bonuses.






The Realm Eye says:











The Sprite World is a dimensional plane found among the stars. The sprites oversee the environmental affairs of the realm from the cosmos.

Sprites themselves are the manifestation of elemental energy. They rarely enter the realm personally, but may appear when nature is threatened.

Since the Sprite World is not meant for corporeal beings, the dimension becomes unstable in the presence of mortals.

Layout



The Sprite World consists of a large number of colorful platforms floating in a starry void, connected by walkways. Multicolored conveyor belts are often found in the rooms, constantly pushing players in their respective direction if stood on. Many of the rooms feature pixel art (clearly visible on the minimap), and almost all rooms are littered with destructible Sprite Tree obstacles.



As players navigate the Sprite World, portals will open around them at random. Each portal will spawn a number of regular or Greater Sprite enemies before disappearing.

Tips and Strategies



The Sprites, while quick, are no big threat. You can easily rush this dungeon, but beware of Native Sprite Gods which can deal a huge amount of damage in certain forms. However, it’s worth killing these as they have a chance of dropping attack potions.



There are always 5 rooms between the starting room and the Limon’s room, not counting either.

History



The Sprite World was one of the first dungeons added in Build 74 (Feb 2010).



In Exalt Version 1.4.0.0 (Mar 2021), this dungeon along with the Deadwater Docks received complete reworks, changing almost all content in the dungeon; historical information about the previous version of the dungeon can be found here.



Before Exalt Version 5.11.0.0 (May 2025), dungeon completion gave 7-15  with 60% chance.



Before Exalt Version 5.12.0.0 (June 2025), dungeon completion gave 20-28 .

  • Due to the ease of farming Sprite Worlds, this dungeon is the most commonly run in the game as of June 2020, with 5,901,859 completions between January and June 2020.
