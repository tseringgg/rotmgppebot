The Puppet Master’s Encore is a high-level dungeon consisting only of a boss fight. It is the only source of the Wooden Box consumable, the UT Prism of Dire Instability, the UT Thousand Shot, and the entire Court Magician Sorcerer ST Set.



The portal to Puppet Master’s Encore has a chance to drop from Shadow Lurker and Organ Harvester. It is also guaranteed to drop from Daughter of Limon, Maze Minotaur and is accessible via the Court of Oryx.



This dungeon must be completed to earn ‘Travel of the Decade‘, ‘Enemy of the Court‘, ‘Hero of the Nexus’ and ‘Realm of the Mad God’ fame bonuses.






The Realm Eye says:











The Puppet Master granted the souls of his puppet collection to the Void Entity in the afterlife, and in return he was brought back much stronger than his former self.

Without any souls, he locked himself in his workshop and brought his four favorite puppets to life with his own enchantments.

The sealed room serves as an asylum for his insanity, leaving him more motivated than ever to collect fresh human souls and restore true life and vibrancy to his creations.

History



The dungeon was originally in development by Kabam but was presumably scrapped as the company redirected resources to its more profitable mobile games. The game was later sold to DECA Games.



This in-development version was opened by a DECA developer for a group of Youtube content creators.

The Puppet Master’s Encore was finished by DECA and released on Patch 27.7.X6 (Oct 2016), along with the Toxic Sewers and The Hive, which represented DECA’s first major content addition to the game.
