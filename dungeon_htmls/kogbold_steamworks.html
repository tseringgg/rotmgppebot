Kogbold Steamworks

The Kogbold Steamworks is an extremely dangerous dungeon and one of the Exaltation dungeons, boosting Vitality, and notably has a 66% chance to grant an additional Exaltation. The dungeon is a source of various Greater Stat Increase Potions, high-level gear, and several Untiered items, Kogbold Inventor Archer Set Shard, Kogbold Pyrotechnician Wizard Set Shard, It is also home to its unique artifact known as Precision Cog, as well as the Kogbold Enhancement Core. The Advanced version of the dungeon is additionally a rare source of dust and engravings.



The portal to the Kogbold Steamworks drops at guaranteed rate from the Aerial Warship and Kogbold Expedition Engine.



This dungeon must be completed to earn ‘Conqueror of the Realm‘, ‘Hero of the Nexus’ and ‘Realm of the Mad God’ fame bonuses.



This dungeon has an advanced version called the Advanced Kogbold Steamworks.






The Realm Eye says:











The Steamworks, home to the Kogbolds, is a city-sized factory which produces all manner of technology unlike anything within the realm.

Within live the Kogbolds, working to produce the secreations under the absolute command of the Factory Control Core residing at its center.

The factory’s emergence within the realm has been signaled by expeditionary forces sent out to survey and subjugate other realm denizens.

Layout



The Kogbold Steamworks is a large dungeon that only has 1 path which starts from the spawn room to the boss room, there are no branching paths possible. The path consists of a randomized series of pre-set room and pathway types, each of which is staffed with enemies and riddled with hazards that must be maneuvered through.



The path will lead to an encounter with the Kogbold Flying Machine that protects the boss room.

History



This dungeon was added in Exalt Version 3.3.0.0 (Dec 2022).



Prior to Exalt Version 3.3.3.0 (Feb 2023), this dungeon had the following map: 
Unlabeled Map Here



Prior to Exalt Version 3.3.3.0 (Feb 2023), all enemies were immune to stun and daze while the Kogbold Worker in particular was immune to paralyze. All small enemies were also immune to paralyze, and enemies and hazards in general gave less XP. High visibility, plumber apprentice, and welder kogbolds were more aggressive, getting much closer to players. Most turrets were invulnerable and immune to stun and stasis save for the Kogbold Laser, and the Steam Turrets had much longer firing and resting times.



Before Exalt Version 5.11.0.0 (May 2025), this dungeon gave 118-276  with 60% chance, 58-136  with 50% chance and 16-38  with 40% chance on completion.



Before Exalt Version 5.12.0.0 (June 2025), this dungeon gave 38-48 , 36-44  and 34-40  on completion.



Before Exalt Version 5.13.0.0 (June 2025), this dungeon gave 42-52 , 48-58  and 50-56  on completion.
